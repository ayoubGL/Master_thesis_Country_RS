how it works

Personalized Rankings of Countries


This is a ENSIAS & UNIBZ research project devoted to personalized rankings and recommendations of countries and cities. Respondent is asked to fill out a brief personal survey, then select certain features they find important in either in original or destination countries and rate a few countries that are offered . Based on these ratings the system would show three lists of potential countries the respondent might be interested in. At the end two brief surveys are offered: to evaluate the recommendations presented and to evaluate the usability of the system.

The respondent can evaluate as many countries as they want (with a minimum of five) .

This Master thesis is undertaken by Ayoub EL Majjodi with the supervision of the project by Lamia Ben Hiba ,Nabil El Ioini and Mehdi Elahi.
