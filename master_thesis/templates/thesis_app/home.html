This is a web page for a Master thesis project of Ayoub EL Majjodi at ENSIAS. This research project aims at building a Recommender System that can learn the preferences of people and suggest to them a list of countries that can match the provided preferences.

After registration, the users can provide their preferences (e.g., in terms of ratings to the countries). The recommender system uses these preferences to generate personalised ranking lists of countries for the registered users.
The users can also help us to improve the system by providing further feedback on the provided recommendations.

This Master thesis is undertaken with supervision of Lamia Ben Hiba ,Nabil El Ioini and Mehdi Elahi.
