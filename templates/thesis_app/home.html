This is a ENSIAS & UNIBZ research project devoted to personalized rankings and recommendations of countries.

Respondent is asked to rate a few countries that are offered based on the geographic region they select. Based on these ratings the system would show three lists of potential countries the respondent might be interested in.

This Master thesis is undertaken by EL MAJJODI Ayoub with the supervision of the project by Nabil El Ioini and Mehdi Elahi .
